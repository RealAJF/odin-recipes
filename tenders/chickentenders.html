Chicken Tenders

A simple yet delicous chicken tenders onlyl using a few ingreidients.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Eggs
  • Corn Flakes
  • Seasoning
  • Hot sauce
  1. Prep Chicken
  2. Get up to two eggs and wisk them up in a small bowl
  3. Crush corn flakes in another bowl
  4. Coat your chicken into the first bowl with the wisked eggs
  5. Transfer your egg covered chicken into the flakes make sure they are coated with the flakes
  6. Pre heat your airfryer to 400 degrees for 10 minutes
  7. Once its heated place your strips inside the air fryer and rotate halfway through or until the fryer tells you to
