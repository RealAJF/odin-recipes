Air Fried Buffalo Chicken

A healthy approach to Buffalo Chicken Breast in the Air Fryer

  • Skinless Chicken Breast
  • Buffalo Sauce
  • Seasoning
  • Cooking Oil

3 Easy Steps

  1. Using a paper towel, dry your chicken breast off. Then add the chicken breast to a large mixing bowl, and pur the buffalo sauce on top.
  2. Preheat your air fryer to 380 F, using the air fryer setting. Add your chicken breats to the air fryer basket, and season withsalt and black pepper. Set the timer for 8 minutes, flip, and air fry for another 5 to 8 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165 F
