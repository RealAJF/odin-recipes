4 Easy Steps

    !-- Change to over to air fryer after test --!
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook Elbow mac in boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooking through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes . Drain.
  2. Melt 2 tbs butter in a sauce pan over medium heat. Stir in Flour to make a roux. Slowly add milk to roux, stirring constantly. Stir in Cheddar and Parmesan cheeses and cook over low heat until cheese is melted and sauce is thick, about 3 minutes. Place mac in large baking dish and pour sauce over macaroini. Stir well.
    3. !-- include details to brown it before adding the milk --!
  3. Melt 2 tbs butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add breadcrumbs and stir until butter is absorbed, 2 to 3 minutes. Spread over mac to cover. Sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake in preheated oven until cheese sauce is hot and breadcrumbs are browned, about 30 minutes.
